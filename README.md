Projects
Meta Ad Performance & Customer Journey Analytics

Analyzed paid advertising campaign data (~52,000 interaction records) to evaluate marketing effectiveness, engagement behavior, and Return on Ad Spend (ROAS). The marketing team faced declining engagement and inconsistent conversions across campaigns but lacked insights into where customers were dropping off.

Performed data cleaning and validation using SQL and Excel to ensure consistent campaign identifiers and reliable metrics. Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to examine engagement patterns, audience behavior, and time-based performance trends.

Defined and analyzed key marketing KPIs including Click-Through Rate (CTR), Cost Per Click (CPC), Conversion Rate, Engagement Rate, and ROAS. Built a customer journey funnel (Awareness → Engagement → Conversion) to identify critical drop-off stages affecting campaign performance.

Developed interactive Power BI dashboards enabling stakeholders to monitor campaign trends, engagement patterns, funnel conversion metrics, and campaign ROI indicators.

Key insights identified campaigns with 30–35% higher engagement among specific audience segments and revealed significant conversion drop-off between the click and lead submission stages. The analysis also highlighted campaigns with high CPC but low conversion efficiency, suggesting opportunities for targeting optimization.

Tools: SQL | Excel | Power BI



Marketing Analytics & Customer Experience Optimization

Analyzed ~38,000 customer engagement and feedback records to investigate declining marketing performance for an online retail platform. Extracted and transformed engagement datasets using SQL and performed KPI analysis across marketing channels.

Conducted sentiment analysis using Python (Pandas & NumPy) to evaluate customer feedback patterns and identify recurring issues influencing customer experience.

Tracked key marketing and customer experience KPIs including Customer Engagement Rate, Conversion Rate, Sentiment Trends, and Campaign Effectiveness Metrics. Built Power BI dashboards to monitor engagement trends, campaign performance fluctuations, and feedback sentiment distribution.

The analysis identified customer experience issues contributing to an 18–22% decline in engagement rates and highlighted recurring feedback related to delivery delays and service responsiveness, supporting targeted improvements in marketing and customer experience strategies.

Tools: SQL | Python | Power BI



Banking Risk & Customer Behavior Analysis

Analyzed ~24,000 banking customer records to identify financial behavior patterns and potential credit risk indicators supporting lending and risk monitoring decisions.

Performed data validation and preprocessing using Excel and SQL, followed by Exploratory Data Analysis in Python to study relationships between income levels, loans, deposits, and credit card usage.

Created derived features such as income bands, engagement indicators, and financial activity metrics. Evaluated risk-related KPIs including Loan-to-Income Ratio, Credit Utilization Rate, and customer deposit patterns.

Developed Power BI dashboards visualizing customer financial segments, credit utilization trends, and loan behavior patterns to support risk monitoring and decision-making processes.

The analysis revealed customer segments with higher loan-to-income ratios linked to increased credit risk and identified patterns where high credit utilization correlated with potential default risk indicators, supporting more informed lending assessments.

Tools: Excel | SQL | Python | Power BI
